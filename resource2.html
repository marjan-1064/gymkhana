Cardio Basics

Boost your endurance and heart health with these tips

Why Cardio Matters

Cardiovascular exercise strengthens your heart and lungs, improves circulation, and helps you burn calories effectively.

Types of Cardio

Popular cardio exercises include running, cycling, swimming, and HIIT workouts. Choose what fits your lifestyle and preferences.

How to Start

Begin with moderate intensity sessions 3 times a week, gradually increasing duration and intensity as your fitness improves.

Safety Tips

Always warm up and cool down, stay hydrated, and listen to your body to avoid injury.

Conclusion

Consistency and variety keep cardio workouts effective and enjoyable.
